README.txt for low energy nuclear physics (version 1.0)
-------------------------------------------------------

genbb_inspector  histograms definition  files  corresponding to  genbb
decay generators  related to low  energy nuclear physics  (double beta
decay, single beta decay, alpha decays...).

- inspector_histos_prompt.conf : histograms for prompt generated particles
- inspector_histos_delayed.conf : histograms for delayed generated particles
